## Too good to not be pinned

- [**How To Ask Questions The Smart Way**](http://www.catb.org/~esr/faqs/smart-questions.html) by Eric Steven Raymond

    Each time when there was a question/idea, checking posts/chats on chat group/BBS, or seeing some stupid questions / appearance of some arrogant-yet-innocent (arroganocent?) on the internet, they always remind me about the afternoon in my first college year, during when I was navigated to this short article by some already-forgotten links (I read the Chinese version, though). Although the whole article is about the rules of asking questions on some "hackers" or geek BBS/chat group/email lists (looks like a confession of hackers), but I think these rules totally should not be limited to some specific areas. *How To Ask Questions The Smart Way* is THE right title.

    Below is a [Chinese version](https://github.com/ryanhanwu/How-To-Ask-Questions-The-Smart-Way/blob/main/README-zh_CN.md) of this article, and a [tranditional Chinese version repository](https://github.com/ryanhanwu/How-To-Ask-Questions-The-Smart-Way).
